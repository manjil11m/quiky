About Quicky

​Quiky is a specialized web utility built for Social Media Managers (SMMs), creative professionals, and digital marketers. Our platform was developed to streamline the content planning workflow by allowing users to visualize posts, stories, and profiles in a realistic environment before they are published.

​We believe that high-quality design should be accessible and frictionless. By providing a clean, distraction-free interface, Quiky eliminates the complexity of traditional design software, enabling users to create professional-grade mockups in seconds. We are committed to maintaining a fast, reliable, and privacy-focused tool for the creative community.


Privacy Policy

At Quiky, data security and user privacy are foundational. Our application is engineered with a privacy-first approach:

  • Local Execution:

    Quicky operates entirely within the user's browser(client-side). No data, images, or input text are transmitted to, stored on, or processed by external servers.

  • No Data Retention:

    Because our tools operate locally, we donot maintain databases of user activity or mockups.

  • Zero Tracking:

    We donot utilize intrusive tracking mechanisms, nor do we require user accounts or password collection to access our features. Your workplace remains entirely private and temporary to your local browser session.
